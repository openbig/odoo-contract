Portal Invitation

Improved portal invitation workflow

The module portal_invitation improves the onboarding of new portal users. The usability of odoo core module was a bit ugly, leading to unclear advises for a new portal user.

Main improvement was done by fixing the email template and the translation related to it.

Define new portal users

At first you have to choose one of the contacts related to a company as a portal user. Therefore you have to activate the boolean field "In portal". If you do this Odoo will send the email for you.

Clear advises how to use portal

The onboarding was fixed, especially the decription inside of the email is much easier to understand and works like it should.

System creates a portal user

Odoo creates also a user, if you select one partner contact from that list.
